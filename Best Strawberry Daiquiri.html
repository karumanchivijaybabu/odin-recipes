Best Strawberry Daiquiri

Feel the flavour of Strawberry....!

A strawberry daiquiri is the perfect way to sweeten up your summer. This boozy frozen cocktail will make you feel like you're on vacation.

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces frozen strawberries
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup lemon juice
  • ⅛ cup lime juice
  • ¾ cup rum
  • ¼ cup lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
  • 6 cups ice, or as needed

Directions

  1. Place frozen strawberries in a blender
  2. Add sugar, lemon juice, and lime juice.
  3. Pour in rum and lemon-lime beverage, then add ice.
  4. Blend until smooth.
